# v59.53.4 — Mascot Share Proof Card

## Purpose

Adds a public-safe Mascot Progression share card so users can share mascot growth without exposing income, real balance, debt details, or private budget numbers.

## Added

- Mascot proof card inside Mascot Progression.
- Share mascot button for X intent.
- Copy mascot progress text.
- Share mascot image through native share, Telegram bot delivery, or PNG download fallback.
- Share image uses existing flat capture hardening for Telegram/Android stability.

- Mascot stage.
- Stage number.
- Level.
- Mascot Power.
- Wallet HP.
- Badge count.
- Power needed to next stage.
